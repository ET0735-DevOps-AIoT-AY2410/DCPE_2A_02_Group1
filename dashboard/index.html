Project Background

Learn more about us! What do we do?

We are Group 1 of DCPE FT 2A02. In line with the Singapore government’s “30 by 30” goal to produce 30% of food locally in Singapore by 2030, a tender has been issued to develop an “Automated Gardening System”. Due to the limited land area allocated for agriculture, the automated system shall support the monitoring and control of a hydroponics system. To simulate this, we are using a Raspberry Pi microcontroller.

This webpage presents a Dashboard which visualizes the exciting data from the different hydroponics system sensors and our Raspberry Pi.
